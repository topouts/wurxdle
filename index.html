How to Play

Guess the drug name (WURxDLE) in 9 tries

  • Each guess must be a valid 10-letter drug name
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the drug name
    • The letter will turn green if that letter is in the drug name and in the correct spot
    • The letter will turn yellow if that letter is in the drug name but in the wrong spot
    • The letter will turn black if that letter is not in the drug name in any spot
  • Refrain from use of electronic resources to solve the WURxDLE
  • Do not refresh page as you are working on the puzzle. If you refresh, your work will be deleted
